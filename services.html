What we provide

Prescription Dispensing

We fill prescriptions from all licensed practitioners quickly and accurately. Our system checks for drug interactions and dosage accuracy on every order. Most prescriptions are ready within 15 minutes.

  • Ready in 15 minutes
  • Interaction checking
  • Insurance accepted
  • Repeat prescription reminders
🩺

Pharmacist Consultations

Our expert pharmacists are available for private one-on-one consultations. Whether you need advice on your current medication, want to understand side effects, or need guidance on over-the-counter products — we're here for you, free of charge.

  • No appointment needed
  • Private consultation room
  • Medication review
  • Chronic disease support
🧪

Health Screening & Lab Tests

We offer a range of on-site health tests including blood pressure monitoring, blood glucose checks, cholesterol screening, and BMI assessment. Results are immediate and confidential.

  • Blood pressure check
  • Blood glucose & HbA1c
  • Cholesterol screening
  • BMI & body composition
🌿

Vitamins & Supplements

Browse our extensive range of vitamins, minerals, herbal supplements, and nutraceuticals. Our pharmacists can recommend the right supplements based on your health goals and current medications.

  • 5,000+ products in stock
  • Pharmacist-guided selection
  • Brand & generic options
  • Loyalty rewards program

Home Delivery

Can't make it to the pharmacy? We offer same-day home delivery for prescriptions and health products within Istanbul. Order online, by phone, or in-store and we'll bring it to you.

  • Same-day delivery
  • Temperature-controlled packaging
  • Tracked delivery
  • Free on orders over ₺200

Vaccination Services

Stay protected with our in-pharmacy vaccination service. We offer flu shots, travel vaccinations, and other routine immunisations administered by trained healthcare professionals.

  • Flu & COVID vaccines
  • Travel vaccinations
  • No appointment required
  • Vaccination records provided
